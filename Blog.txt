There are 9 Legendary Armaments in Elden Ring. Collecting all of them unlocks a trophy/achievement. However, one of them—the Bolt of Gransax—is permanently missable.

If you defeat Maliketh and turn Leyndell into the "Ashen Capital" before picking up the Bolt, it disappears from the game world. You have to start New Game+ to get it.

Other weapons, like the Eclipse Shotel, are hidden in obscure corners of the map.

Otagon acts as your trophy tracker. It scans your inventory to see which ones you have and guides you to the ones you are missing before you trigger the point of no return.

Top 3 Missable Weapons

The Issue: It sits on the giant dragon spear in Leyndell.

The Deadline: You MUST grab it before killing Maliketh in Farum Azula.

2. Devourer's Scepter

The Issue: Dropped by Recusant Bernahl.

The Complexity: If you complete his questline, he invades you in Farum Azula. If you kill the area boss (Maliketh) before he invades, he might not spawn.

3. Golden Order Greatsword

The Issue: Hidden in the Cave of the Forlorn.

The Difficulty: The cave is in the Consecrated Snowfield, requiring the Haligtree Medallion to access.

If you equip a bleed weapon and heavy armor, your Mimic becomes a tanky bleed machine. It even uses items in your hotbar (like Raw Meat Dumplings) to heal itself.

The Issue: It's locked behind a Stonesword Key door in Night's Sacred Ground.

The Issue: "I can't summon it."

The Mechanic: Mimic Tear costs 660 HP to summon, not FP. If your Vigor is too low, the summon fails.

These bosses drop "Remembrances" which grant powerful weapons. Missing them means missing out on the full experience (and the Heavy Thrusting Sword "Dragon King's Cragblade").

Otagon acts as your secrets map. It highlights the drop-down points and obscure interactions required to trigger these cinematic fights.

The Location: Farum Azula.

The Secret: You have to backtrack from the "Beside the Great Bridge" grace, take an elevator down, run past enemies, and jump off the cliff onto floating rocks. Then lie down in a grave slot.

The Location: Deeproot Depths.

The Secret: You must complete Fia's questline (The Deathbed Companion). Give her the Cursemark of Death, then enter the Deathbed Dream.

The Location: Subterranean Shunning-Grounds (Leyndell Sewers).

The Secret: Navigating the maze of pipes to find the bottom of the world.
